    📊 The Trap of Probability and Lotto Psychology

    We often forget the mathematical concept of "independent events". Just because number 1 came out last week doesn't mean the probability of number 1 coming out this week decreases. Even if heads came up 10 times in a coin toss, the probability of heads coming up on the 11th toss is still 50%.

    However, if we look at the 'Law of Large Numbers', the story changes a bit. As the number of trials approaches infinity, the frequency of appearance of all numbers should eventually become similar. At this point, many analysts build a strategy of betting on 'Cold Numbers' that haven't appeared for a long time.

    Does a Lucky Spot Exist?

    The secret of the lucky store where many 1st prize winners came out is actually simple. It is because 'they sell a lot'. If the sales volume is 10 times higher than other places, the probability of a winner appearing is also 10 times higher. But this does not increase your individual winning probability. What is more important than where you buy is which number combination you choose.

    ⏱️ Brain Reaction Speed and Aging Curve

    Human reaction speed refers to the time it takes for visual information to be transmitted to the brain through the retina and for the brain to recognize it and give a command to the muscles. Usually, it peaks in the early 20s and gradually slows down thereafter. This is why professional gamers worry about the 'aging curve' even in their mid-20s.

    How to Improve Reaction Speed

    But it's too early to be disappointed. Thanks to the brain's 'Neuroplasticity', you can maintain or improve reaction speed through training. 1. Sufficient Sleep: An essential condition for restoring the brain's processing speed. 2. Aerobic Exercise: Increases blood flow to the brain to improve cognitive function. 3. Fast Judgment Games: Repeating FPS games or reaction speed tests like this is also excellent training to stimulate neural circuits.
